Privacy Policy - Personal Smart Spend

Effective Date: February 24, 2026
Last Updated: February 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Personal Smart Spend (the "App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the Personal Smart Spend mobile application (package: com.personalsmartspend.app).

Personal Smart Spend is a privacy-first personal finance app. Your financial records are designed to stay on your device unless you explicitly export, back up, or share them.

Summary
  • Your financial records (transactions, budgets, goals, categories, notes, etc.) are stored locally on your device.
  • We do not upload your financial records to our servers.
  • The App may use third-party services (such as Google AdMob, Firebase Analytics, and Firebase Crashlytics) to provide ads, measure app usage, and improve stability, subject to your settings and consent where required.
  • You can manage privacy and consent choices inside the App (Settings > Privacy & Consent).

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Personal Smart Spend mobile app and our official hosted privacy policy page.

2. Information We Collect

A. Financial and app data you enter (stored locally on your device)

This may include:

  • Income and expense records
  • Categories, notes, budgets, goals, recurring schedules
  • Transfers and account-related entries
  • App preferences and settings
  • Reminder settings
  • Security preferences (for example app lock configuration)
  • Backup/export preferences

We do not collect or store this financial data on our servers.

B. Limited technical and usage data (via third-party SDKs/services)

Depending on your settings, consent choices, device configuration, and applicable law, third-party services used by the App may collect limited information such as:

  • App crash reports
  • App performance and diagnostics data
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App version
  • Basic usage information (for example screen views or feature usage)
  • Advertising identifiers or similar identifiers (where available)
  • Ad interaction and ad measurement data
  • Consent-related status (for ad/analytics choices)

This data does not include the financial records you enter into the App.

C. Support communications

If you contact us by email, we may receive:

  • Your email address
  • The contents of your message
  • Any information you choose to include in the message or attachments

3. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Operate app features (locally on your device)
  • Show and measure ads in the free version
  • Diagnose crashes and improve stability
  • Understand general feature usage and improve the App
  • Store and honor your privacy/consent choices
  • Provide support when you contact us

We do not use your financial records for advertising targeting.

4. Local-First Data Handling

Personal Smart Spend is designed so that your financial records remain on your device by default.

The App may create or manage local files when you use features like:

  • Backup and restore
  • CSV export
  • PDF reports
  • File sharing (when you choose to share a file)

If you export or share files, those files may be stored or transmitted by the apps/services you choose (for example email, cloud storage, messaging apps). Their privacy policies will apply.

5. Ads, Analytics, and Crash Reporting

The App may use third-party SDKs/services, including:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Firebase Analytics (optional analytics/performance usage insights)
  • Firebase Crashlytics (optional crash reporting)
  • Google Play Services / related Google mobile services

Consent and controls

Where required by law (for example GDPR/EEA/UK or certain U.S. state privacy frameworks), the App may show a consent form and privacy options for advertising.

You can manage privacy controls in the App, including analytics/performance collection, crash reporting, ad personalization preferences, and ad consent review/refresh (where supported).

6. Premium Features and Payments

As of February 24, 2026, Premium purchasing is not enabled in the current release of Personal Smart Spend.

If in-app purchases or subscriptions are added in a future release:

  • Payments will be processed by the applicable platform billing provider (for example Google Play)
  • We will not collect or store your payment card details

7. Permissions and Device Access

The App may request access to certain device features/permissions to provide features you choose to use, such as:

  • Notifications: for reminders and alerts
  • Biometric/Fingerprint authentication: for app lock/security features
  • Photos/Media/Files: for user-initiated file picking, imports, exports, or media selection (where applicable)
  • Network/Internet access: for ads, consent flows, analytics/crash reporting, and related service connectivity

Permissions may vary by Android version and device.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your financial records.

We may share limited technical/usage data with third-party service providers and partners only as needed to support:

  • Advertising and ad measurement
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Crash diagnostics
  • Security, fraud prevention, and service reliability

These providers process data under their own privacy policies and terms.

9. Data Retention

Local app data

Your local financial and app data remains on your device until you delete it in the App, reset a profile, uninstall the App, or replace it using backup/restore features.

Third-party telemetry/ads data

Retention of technical/usage data collected by third-party services is governed by their policies.

Exported/shared files

Files you export or share are retained wherever you save/share them, subject to the policies of those services and your own actions.

10. Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect data and app functionality, including features such as local-only data storage for financial records, optional app lock/device authentication integration, and optional encrypted backups (where enabled).

No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. International Data Processing

If you use the App outside your home country, third-party services (such as ad/analytics/crash providers) may process limited technical/usage data in other countries under their own privacy practices.

Your financial records entered in the App are intended to remain local on your device unless you choose to export/share them.

12. Children's Privacy

Personal Smart Spend is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable law in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through our own servers.

If you believe a child has provided personal information to us via support email, contact us and we will review and delete it where appropriate.

13. Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding personal information (for example access, deletion, correction, or consent withdrawal).

In Personal Smart Spend, you can generally:

  • Manage analytics, crash reporting, and ad personalization settings in the App
  • Review/refresh ad consent options (where available)
  • Delete local data by removing records, resetting a profile, or uninstalling the App
  • Control notifications through device settings
  • Limit ad tracking or reset advertising identifiers using device/Google settings (where available)
